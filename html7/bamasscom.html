BA Mass Comm

Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication is a three-year undergraduate program equipping students with the technical skills and social understanding to write about and convey information - to the general public - involving business,culture, budget issues and sports, through the means of mass communication such as TV, radio, and web-based social networks. As the field of journalism is established on truth, the course provides a holistic knowledge at both theoretical and practical level.
